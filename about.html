About Greenfield Academy

Our story, mission, and values

Our story

Greenfield Academy was founded in 2015 by a group od educators who believed that every child deserves access to quality education. What started as a small school with just 50 students has grown into one of the most respected educational institutions in the West Coast Region, now serving over 850 students.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops the whold child - academically, socially, and morally - preparing them to be responsible citizens and future leaders.

Our Valuse

  • Excellence - We strive for the highest standards in everything we do
  • Integrity - We teach honesty and ethical behavior
  • Respect - We value every individual and their contribution
  • Community - We work together as school family

Our Facilities

  • Modern classrooms with learning resources
  • Computer laboratory
  • Science laboratory
  • Library and reading room
  • Sports field and playground
  • Cafeteria
